APJ Abdul Kalam

15 October 1931- 27 July 2015

"If you want to shine like a sun,first burn like a sun"

Biography

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an Indian scientist and the 11th President. Born in Rameswaram, he overcame financial challenges to become an aeronautical engineer. His work with ISRO and DRDO bolstered India's space and defense capabilities. Elected President in 2002, he focused on education and youth empowerment. After his term, he continued inspiring through speeches and books like "Wings of Fire." Kalam passed away in 2015, leaving a legacy of dedication to science, education, and the empowerment of the youth.
